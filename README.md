# BIG SHARKS LTD

**A Trusted Gate to Business Investment**

BIG SHARKS LTD is a trust-first business investment platform focused on carefully vetted Nigerian small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs). The platform emphasizes transparency, disciplined evaluation, and conservative governance.

---

## Current Status

BIG SHARKS LTD is currently operating in **Phase 1 (Nigeria-only)**.

- This repository hosts the **public informational website**
- Public waiting list registration is open
- **No contributions are being accepted at this stage**
- No investment products or returns are being offered

Progress updates are communicated through published transparency reports.

---

## Repository Purpose

This repository contains the static website for BIG SHARKS LTD, built for clarity, speed, and institutional credibility.

### Included:
- Public pages (Home, How It Works, Vetting, Contact)
- Transparency reports
- Compliance documents:
  - Terms of Service
  - Privacy Policy
  - Risk Disclosure

### Excluded:
- No payment processing
- No contribution handling
- No user accounts
- No backend services
