Vetting Process

BIG SHARKS LTD is conservative and trust-first. Pilot businesses are evaluated carefully. During Phase 1, identities may remain anonymous publicly until approval.

Phase 1 Focus

BIG SHARKS LTD begins with 2–3 pilot businesses in the following sectors: Farming, Food Manufacturing, and Logistics. The purpose of Phase 1 is to establish trust through disciplined evaluation and verified reporting.

1. Intake & Screening

We review basic eligibility, operating history, and sector alignment. Businesses that are not operating or cannot provide basic documentation are not considered.

2. Operational Review

We assess management discipline, operations, customer demand, and execution ability. We prioritize integrity and clarity over promises.

3. Financial Review

We examine revenue patterns, costs, obligations, and use-of-funds plans. If records are unclear, the application may be paused or rejected.

4. Risk Assessment

We document sector and business risks openly. This includes supply chain risk, market risk, regulatory risk, and management risk.

5. Approval Decision

Only businesses that meet conservative standards proceed. Approval requires willingness to operate under transparency and reporting expectations.

6. Public Disclosure

When a pilot is approved, we prepare a public profile with clear disclosures. Until then, the business may be listed anonymously (e.g., “Pilot Business A”).

Important Notice

Vetting reduces risk, but it does not eliminate risk. Returns are not guaranteed. BIG SHARKS LTD publishes verified updates through transparency reports.
