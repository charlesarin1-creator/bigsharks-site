At a glance

We build credibility by constraints, verification, and clear public disclosure.

What we are: A gatekeeper process for disciplined SME evaluation and governance oversight.

What we are not: Not a get-rich platform. Not a regulated fund. Not a guarantee of returns.

Focus sectors (Phase 1): Farming, food manufacturing, logistics.

Pilot scope: 2–3 pilot businesses, depth over breadth.

Compliance posture: Any future participation model will be structured with legal and regulatory counsel.

Our Doctrine

Restraint over hype. Verification over persuasion. Transparency over pressure.

Trust-first governance

We communicate constraints clearly, disclose risks early, and publish factual updates. Trust is earned through structure.

  • Clear disclosures and operational limits
  • Disciplined evaluation and documentation
  • Public reporting milestones

Disciplined evaluation

Businesses must pass defined gates. Weak records do not pass. This reduces noise and protects seriousness.

  • Documentation and compliance checks
  • Financial and operational review
  • Management interview and site checks

Transparency-led progress

We publish reports that describe decisions, risks, and milestones—so observers can verify how the gate works.

  • Weekly transparency reports
  • Clear risk statements
  • Documented governance actions
